Vacation Boarding Service

  Many dog owners face difficulty to leave their dog at home when they are going to holidays with their
  family or friends. Most of the time, the dog owners will be worried about their safety and hygiene
  while they are away from their pet dog. Well there is nothing to be worried because PAW’s Life
  offers vacation boarding for your beloved dogs with the care of our trained team to ensure your loved
  ones to be comfortable and happy during their stay at our centre. We will also take your dog out for
  outdoor bathroom breaks and play time throughout the day. On the other hand, we also give you the
  option to schedule additional playtime for you dog in our spacious centre. Moreover, our facility is
  also monitored 24 hours every day and our team is trained in dog’s behaviour, pet first aid and CPR to
  ensure their safety. This will ensure your dog will be happy and healthy when you return.



    Why Choose Us ?

  • Webcam Access

    •   We offer webcam access to our dog parents while they are out on a vacation
      because leaving our fury loved ones can be a tense experience to endure. Our dog parents can watch
      their loved ones playing and having a good time with other dogs in our centre whenever they want
      through computer or mobile device.

  • Safety Guaranteed

    •   One of our PAW’s Care main motto is to ensure the safety of our clients dogs
      from any harm of ticks, fleas and other dangerous illnesses. We make sure all the dogs that are
      appointed for vacation boarding to be vaccinated and free of fleas, ticks and other contagious
      sickness.

  • SPA Services

    •   We also offer spa services if you want to pamper your dog with extra charges of RM 70
      only as a part of our vacation boarding packages. This spa service will include nail cutting, bathing
      and fur trimming.

    Requirement before signing up your dog for vacation boarding

  • We recommend the dog’s parent to vaccinations of their dog administered 10 days before
    the check in or at least 2 days before the schedule of the vacation boarding.

  • We will check for ticks, fleas and other contagious illnesses on the dog before bringing them
    into the care centre to avoid it spread to other dogs.

  • We encourage the dog’s parent to bring their dog’s own bedding and food to avoid any
    complication during the stay.

    Price rate

  • RM 15 per day during weekdays excluding SPA Service

  • RM 18 per day during weekends excluding SPA Service
