About Us

In the year 2000, a group of five friends started running this business together. Their names are Rizwan Ahmed, Kajendaren,
Gauthaman, Goh Jin Wei and Chong Kai Ping. These five have spent their entire childhood watching animals
getting abused and abandoned by them owners, so what they did was these friends started their own adoption and care home
for these poor animals and slowly over the years it has become the leading pet care service. This company
has handled many types of pets such as birds, cats, hamsters and especially dogs. As years pass the company has introduce
new services. From adoption homes, to vets and now the newly opened a dog grooming training and boarding home
