Grooming

Our goal is to provide your dog with a quality grooming service in a safe and relaxing environment free from cages and drying cabinets and of course at great prices. We take the best possible care of your dog using top of the range products in our unrivalled dog grooming salons. Our trained dog groomers are focused on animal welfare and delivering the best possible quality service. Our Salons are based in two locations, Malaysia and Singapore, to provide the best dog grooming London has to offer.
